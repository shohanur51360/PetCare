What to Expect

Apply

To start our intake process, please fill out the new
client form here. Once we receive your
application, we will immediately begin looking
into our availability and email you within 24

Consultation

The initial Meet ‘n Greet will take place via Zoom
so you can show us where all the necessary
supplies are located, all while keeping a safe
distance.
